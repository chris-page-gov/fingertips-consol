2. Specification: Fingertips analytics & monitoring 

1.0 Specification 

1.1 Purpose:  

- To be aware of public health related indicators which are: 

- Trending in undesired direction 

- Trending the in desired direction 

- Currently performing statistically worse that England 

- Currently performing statistically better that England 

- Report the above by the geographies of Warwickshire as a whole and for each district and borough. 

1.2 Scope 

- All Fingertips indicators available at Warwickshire geography and / or District and Boroughs 

1.3 Definitions of Categories for Surveillance 

- All categories on Fingertips, could be ordered by Fingertips profile 

1.4 Information Governance 

- Fingertips is publicly available and therefore IG considerations are minimal 

1.5 UX 

- Create two reports, one for those that are doing well and one that require focus. 

1.6 Data Ingestion 

- Use the Fingertips API
